The case

Jordan, 19, is finishing a transition program. Strong visual-spatial reasoning, hyperfocuses on systems and how things fit together, finds open-ended verbal prompts overwhelming, communicates more reliably in writing than aloud, has been told for years what they struggle with and rarely what they're good at.

Redesign sprint

Same pod. Work through all four lenses below together — don't split them up, the friction between them is the point. Then redesign one piece of a course around Jordan's profile.

Four lenses — talk through each

Big picture

If we threw out the current format entirely, what would this assignment look like built around Jordan from scratch?

Detail check

What's the specific moment in the current version where Jordan would most likely get stuck or shut down?

Synthesis

Of everything said so far, what's the one change that matters most?

Communication

How will we describe this redesign to a colleague in two sentences?
